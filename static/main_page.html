About Meta Consult

Meta Consult is an innovative AI-powered business consultancy platform designed to revolutionize how companies approach problem-solving and strategic planning. We leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence and multi-agent workflows to provide comprehensive, data-driven solutions tailored to your specific business challenges.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize access to high-quality business consultancy by combining the power of artificial intelligence with deep domain expertise. We help businesses of all sizes identify problems, analyze root causes, and develop actionable solutions that drive growth, optimize operations, and enhance competitive positioning.

Key Benefits

  • Intelligent Problem Analysis: Our AI agents conduct thorough analysis of your business challenges, identifying underlying issues and their interconnected impacts.
  • Multi-Agent Collaboration: Specialized AI agents work together, each focusing on different aspects like strategic planning, market research, technology solutions, and management strategies.
  • Comprehensive Solutions: Receive detailed, actionable solution reports backed by real-world research and best practices from across industries.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Leverage web search capabilities to incorporate the latest market trends, industry benchmarks, and successful case studies into your solutions.
  • Streamlined Workflow: From initial conversation to final solution report, our agentic workflow automates complex processes, saving time and ensuring consistency.
  • Personalized Approach: Every solution is tailored to your company's specific context, industry, and unique challenges.

Agentic Workflow

Meta Consult employs a sophisticated multi-agent workflow that transforms your business conversations into comprehensive solutions:

  1. Conversation Phase: Our AI consultant engages in detailed conversations to understand your business challenges, asking targeted questions to gather comprehensive information.
  2. Proposal Generation: Once sufficient information is gathered, our Proposal Agent generates a structured problem analysis document that outlines the issues, their root causes, and impacts across financial, operational, and strategic dimensions.
  3. Domain Classification: The Planner Agent analyzes your proposal using advanced reasoning to classify it into relevant strategic domains (Strategic Planning, Market Research, Technology, Management, etc.).
  4. Web Search & Research: Specialized Web Search Agents search the internet for solutions, case studies, and best practices in the identified domains, gathering actionable insights from across industries.
  5. Solution Generation: Our AI synthesizes all gathered information to create a comprehensive solution report with implementation roadmaps, risk mitigation strategies, and expected ROI.
  6. Document Delivery: Receive your solution as a professionally formatted PDF document ready for implementation.

How Meta Consult Works

How It Works

Meta Consult leverages a sophisticated multi-agent AI system to transform your business challenges into actionable solutions. Here's how our unique workflow operates:

1. Initial Consultation

Begin by chatting with our AI consultant about your business challenges. The system asks targeted questions to understand your company's context, industry, specific problems, and desired outcomes. This conversation phase ensures we have comprehensive information before moving to analysis.

2. Problem Analysis & Proposal Generation

After gathering sufficient information (typically after 5+ exchanges), our Proposal Agent automatically generates a detailed problem analysis document. This document includes:

  • Executive Summary of the problem
  • Detailed Problem Statement
  • Key Reasons and Root Causes
  • Financial Impact Analysis
  • Operational Impact Assessment
  • Strategic Impact Evaluation

3. Domain Classification

Our Planner Agent uses advanced AI reasoning to analyze your proposal and classify it into one or more strategic domains:

  • Strategic Planning: Long-term vision, growth strategies, competitive positioning
  • Market Research: Market analysis, customer insights, competitive intelligence
  • Technology Solutions: Digital transformation, IT strategy, automation
  • Management: Organizational structure, process optimization, team management

4. Web Search & Research

Once domains are identified, specialized Web Search Agents automatically search the internet for:

  • Industry best practices and case studies
  • Successful solutions implemented by similar companies
  • Latest trends and innovations in relevant domains
  • Tools, technologies, and methodologies applicable to your challenge

5. Solution Generation

Our AI synthesizes all gathered information—your conversation, problem analysis, domain classification, and web research—to generate a comprehensive solution report. This report includes:

  • Executive Summary with key recommendations
  • Detailed solution breakdown with implementation phases
  • Step-by-step implementation roadmap
  • Risk mitigation strategies
  • Expected results and ROI analysis

What Makes Us Different

  • Multi-Agent Architecture: Unlike single-AI systems, we use specialized agents that collaborate, ensuring comprehensive coverage of all aspects of your challenge.
  • Real-World Research Integration: Our solutions are grounded in actual case studies and industry practices, not just theoretical knowledge.
  • Automated Workflow: The entire process from conversation to solution is automated, ensuring consistency and speed while maintaining quality.
  • Personalized Context: Every solution is tailored to your specific company, industry, and challenges, not generic templates.
  • Transparent Process: You can see each stage of the workflow as it progresses, understanding how your solution is being developed.
